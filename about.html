About Us

Nidhi Path Loan Ventures

Nidhi Path Loan Ventures is built to guide students, families, salaried professionals and business owners through loan decisions with clarity, trust and end-to-end support.

Who We Are

A student-first education-loan guidance partner for families planning bigger journeys

We help students and parents understand education-loan possibilities with calm, practical consultation. From study abroad loans discussions to documentation, EMI awareness, co-applicant preparation and multiple bank guidance, our work is built around trust, clarity and responsible financial decisions.

Our Mission

Guide every education decision with clarity and confidence.

Company Purpose

Financial guidance that feels clear, local and accountable

Loan customers often face the same problems: unclear eligibility, scattered documents, confusing repayment terms and uncertainty about which option suits their profile. Nidhi Path works as a guidance partner, helping clients understand requirements, prepare documents and move forward with a practical application plan.

For studentsAdmission-based education loan guidance for India and abroad.
For familiesParent or co-applicant support, repayment discussion and document preparation.
For professionalsPersonal, home and financial solution consultation based on profile.
For businessesBusiness loans discussion with income, banking and documentation focus.
Clarity

Simple explanation

We explain the loan path, possible document needs, repayment basics and application steps in customer-friendly language.

Preparation

Document preparation

We help customers prepare KYC, admission proof, academic records, income documents, bank statements and relevant supporting papers.

Coordination

Process support

We support customer follow-up and communication clarity while respecting that final decisions rest with multiple banks.
