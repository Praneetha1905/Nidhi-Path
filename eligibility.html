Eligibility

Loan Eligibility & Document Preparation

Use this page as a practical preparation checklist before speaking with our team. It combines education-loan document planning, other loan requirements and an advanced EMI planner for first-level financial clarity.

For Education Loan

  • Student admission proof
  • Academic records
  • Co-applicant or parent details
  • Income proof if applicable
  • KYC documents
  • Bank statements if required

For Other Loans

  • KYC documents
  • Income proof
  • Bank statements
  • Property or business documents based on loan type
Student Profile

Admission and academic preparation

Keep admission letter or offer proof, course duration, fee structure, academic records and entrance result details ready where applicable.

Co-applicant

Parent or guardian details

Education loans commonly require parent or co-applicant information, KYC, bank statements and income details based on the bank process.

Repayment

EMI and moratorium planning

Plan repayment using tenure, interest rate, down payment, processing fee and education moratorium assumptions before the formal application.

Other Loans

Income and credit profile

For personal, home and business loans, eligibility commonly depends on income, obligations, credit history, bank statements and loan purpose.
