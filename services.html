Service Method

Every enquiry follows a clear support structure

Nidhi Path does not position loan guidance as a one-click promise. The team first understands the customer profile, then checks preparation, explains possible documents, discusses repayment impact and supports the next formal process step.

Requirement map Purpose, amount, urgency, city, income type and applicant profile.
Eligibility preparation Age, income, co-applicant, credit history and document availability.
Cost awareness EMI, tenure, processing fees, margin money and total repayment estimate.
Follow-up support Practical reminders, document gaps and organized lead tracking.
